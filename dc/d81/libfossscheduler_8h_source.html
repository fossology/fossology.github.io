60  * The following macro definitions are meant to act as their own statement in
61  * the c language. To accomplish this, they needed to not only be able to be used
62  * in the situation of an "if" statement with no body, but also require that
63  * they are followed by a ";".
64  *
65  * To do this the "do {} while(0)" loop is used, the loop will not appear in
66  * result flow control since it does not modify the flow of control, but it is
